About Me:

Born in the 80’s, with a love of power ballads, and all things My Little Pony, I’m Keegan Ariel. I am a newbie programer who is really excited to learn as many things as i can about how to be the best programer I can be! I am really excited about learning Javascript and to discover how much I can do with coding.

My Home:

I was born on at home in Portland Oregon. My parents were hippies and raised me with lots of strong ideals and a DIY mentality. This city is growing up so fast that I often feel like I’m a kind of unicorn around here lately as it is pretty rare to find other people who actually grew up here.

Fun Facts:

Dream Job:

Important Things:

Music is a driving force in my life, making it and consuming other people's. I also love making new connections with people and I try to learn something new every day!
